# AccoPlacers User Manual

Welcome to AccoPlacers — the premium recruitment platform for top-tier professionals and employers in the GCC. This manual provides a step-by-step guide to using the platform effectively.

---

## 👤 For Candidates (Job Seekers)

### 1. Registration & Profile Creation
- **Step 1: Basic Info**: Provide your name and email. Note that we use a "Honeypot" security system; simply fill the visible fields.
- **Step 2: Security**: Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters).
- **Step 3: Professional Profile**: Select your role, location, and experience. 
- **AI Resume Upload**: Upload your PDF resume. Our AI parser will automatically extract your skills and experience to populate your profile with 99% accuracy.

### 2. Candidate Dashboard
- **Profile Score**: Monitor your "Profile Completion" percentage. A higher score increases your visibility to premium employers.
- **Next Best Step**: Follow the AI-suggested actions (e.g., "Jump to Skills") to improve your profile quality.
- **Application Tracker**: Track your status from "Submitted" to "Contacted" in real-time.

### 3. Job Board & Expressing Interest
- **Browse Jobs**: View all active openings curated for your role.
- **Job Details**: Click any job card to view the full description and company details.
- **Express Interest**: Click the "Express Interest" button. This signals the employer that you are interested, moving you into their "Shortlisted" queue.

---

## 🏢 For Employers (Recruiters)

### 1. Employer Registration
- **Company Profile**: Provide your business details and industry.
- **Brand Identity**: Upload your company logo. This will be visible to candidates on all your job postings.
- **Account Verification**: New accounts are reviewed to maintain platform quality.

### 2. Recruiter Command Center (Dashboard)
- **Talent Search**: Use the powerful filter system to find candidates by location, role, or experience.
- **AI Vector Search**: Search using natural language (e.g., "Senior Auditor with IFRS knowledge") to find semantic matches that traditional keyword searches might miss.
- **Premium Talent Pool**: View "Featured" candidates who have been vetted for high-priority roles.

### 3. Job Management
- **Post a Job**: Create detailed job listings including salary ranges and specific requirements.
- **Manage Listings**: Edit or delete your active jobs directly from the dashboard.
- **Interest Tracking**: See which candidates have expressed interest in your roles.

### 4. Subscription & Payments
- **Upgrade to Premium**: Access advanced search features and priority candidate placement.
- **Secure Payments**: All transactions are handled securely via Stripe. Your plan will update automatically once payment is confirmed.

---

## 🔒 Security & Best Practices
- **Password Safety**: Never share your login credentials.
- **Verified Status**: Always ensure your profile is up-to-date. Employers prioritize "Verified" profiles.
- **Browser Compatibility**: For the best experience, use the latest version of Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

**Support**: For any issues, contact us via the landing page contact form.
