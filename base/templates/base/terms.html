2. No Job Guarantee

Registration with Accoplacers does not guarantee a job offer, employment, or interview selection.

The final decision regarding shortlisting, interviewing, and hiring rests entirely with the respective employers.

The candidate is solely responsible for qualifying in interviews and securing job offers.

3. Registration Fees

A non-refundable registration fee is required to complete the registration process.

The collected fee is used for:

  • Providing job opportunity notifications
  • Arranging or coordinating interviews with potential employers
  • Covering administrative and communication costs

No refund will be issued under any circumstances, including:

  • Non-selection for interviews or jobs

4. Success-Based Service Charge

Accoplacers provides job interview coordination and placement assistance services to help candidates connect with genuine employers.

Upon receiving a confirmed job offer letter through Accoplacers’ assistance, the candidate agrees to pay a success-based service charge equivalent to up to 30–40% of their first month’s gross salary.

This payment is made only after the candidate receives the official offer letter from the employer and confirms their acceptance of the position.

The service charge is a contribution towards the effort, time, and resources used for:

  • Identifying suitable job openings
  • Coordinating interviews with verified employers
  • Providing continuous communication and support throughout the selection process

Accoplacers does not collect any placement fee in advance, and the service charge applies only after successful placement.

Candidates will receive a proper receipt for every payment made to ensure full transparency.
